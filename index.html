Nouns Colombia

Arte · Cultura · Web3 · Cali, Colombia ⌐◨-◨

Nouns Colombia es una comunidad que lleva la cultura nounish a Colombia — ferias de arte gráfico, murales colaborativos, encuentros IRL y un programa de residencia artística financiado por Nouns DAO. Lo que sucede aquí es replicable en cualquier lugar del mundo.

Programa de Residencia Artística

En 2024, Nouns DAO aprobó la Prop #840 — una residencia artística para un creador en Colombia. El modelo: financiamiento directo desde el DAO, libertad creativa total, y anclaje en la escena cultural local.

La primera residente es Nadiecito, pixel artista, ilustradora y gestora cultural de Cali. Su trabajo está documentado en la crónica de esta página — y su historia es solo el comienzo.

NOC — Noun O'Clock Contributor

Noun O'Clock (NOC) is a cultural moment in web3 celebrating the daily "birth" of each new Noun. Every 24 hours a Noun is auctioned — that exact moment is marked by artwork from contributors worldwide.

Nadiecito has contributed 100+ animations to the NOC channel on Farcaster — self-described as "almost daily, barely animated pieces" — each one a daily beat of onchain culture.

Proyecto IRL · Dic 2024

Mural "Amar y ya" — 60m²

Un mural colaborativo de 15m × 4m en Cali, creado junto a 3 artistas locales entre el 11 y el 21 de diciembre de 2024. El tema: personas en situación de calle y sus vínculos profundos con animales compañeros.

La frase "Amar y ya" (To love, and that's it) suena como "Amarilla" (Yellow) — amor incondicional, sin condiciones.

Los fondos de la residencia aseguraron una pared de 15m²; la colaboración con Yellow Collective (Prop #18 Executed) la expandió a 60m² totales. La inauguración del 21 de diciembre reunió a la comunidad: empanadas, lulada, y una colecta de 28kg de alimento + 5 bolsas para la Fundación Familia Cuatro Huellas.

Residency Log

Chronicle ⌐◨-◨

May 22, 2024 Origin

Global Pizza Party — First Nouns Amigos Collab · Medellín

Organized the Global Pizza Party at Surco Records, Medellín, in collaboration with Nouns Amigos (the Latin American SubDAO). That first direct collaboration sparked everything — started attending Nouns Amigos calls, diving deeper into the ecosystem, and discovering Farcaster.

📸
📸
📸
2024 Origin

Farcaster, Pixel Art & the NOC Channel

Discovered Farcaster and the dynamics of Nouns channels. Pixel art clicked immediately — Nouns was the perfect place to apply it and have it valued. Started contributing daily to the NOC (Noun O'Clock) channel: 100+ animations, self-titled "almost daily, barely animated pieces." Also participated in Nouns Rounds, Flows (sunseted 2025), and IRL Nouns Town meetups.

2024 Milestone

Abacus v1 — First On-Chain Proposal (Prop #641, Defeated)

Submitted the first Nouns head to DAO governance — Abacus v1 (Prop #641). It didn't pass, but the experience set the path for 14 more proposals to follow and a relentless belief in the process.

Aug 2024 Milestone

Prop #840 Approved — Residency Begins ⌐◨-◨

Nouns DAO votes YES. Scope: open experimentation, content creation, pixel art, IRL events, and collaboration with local artists in Cali. Period: Aug 2024 → Mar 2025.

Nov 17–21, 2024 Event

Devconnect Argentina — Nouns Booth, Buenos Aires

Participated as resident artist at the Nouns booth for 5 days. Created live pixel art pieces printed on a risograph machine on the spot. First time meeting the global Nouns community in person — many people known only online, finally face to face.

Feria Gráfica Nounish — Primera Edición · Cali

First edition of the Nounish Graphic Fair, funded by Nouns Amigos. Local artists with their own stands, each designing a nounish-inspired sticker. The foundation of what became a recurring community gathering. Full archive at ferianounish.xyz ↗

Dec 11–21, 2024 Event · IRL 2/4

Mural Amarillo "Amar y ya" — Cali · 60m²

Collaborative mural started Dec 11, inaugurated Dec 21. 15m wide × 4m high = 60m² total surface. Created with 3 local Cali artists. The theme: people experiencing homelessness and their deep bonds with animal companions. The phrase "Amar y ya" (To love, and that's it) sounds like "Amarilla" (Yellow) in Spanish — unconditional love.

Collaboration with Yellow Collective (Prop #18 Executed). Residency funds secured a 15m² wall; Yellow Collective partnership made it 60m². Dec 21 inauguration: shared empanadas & lulada, collected 28kg pet food + 5 bags for Fundación Familia Cuatro Huellas.

ETH Denver & ETH Boulder — OnchainCreators

May 5, 2025 Milestone

Prop #793 — NOC Decentralized Content Creation Trial

On-chain proposal for a decentralized content creation model for the NOC channel — formalizing the daily artistic practice around Noun O'Clock. Part of a larger vision to sustain and grow the contributor culture.

Jul 5, 2025 Milestone ✓

Abacus Head Executed — Prop #922 ✓ · In the Collection Forever

After multiple proposals and iterations, the Abacus Head (v2) is voted in and executed on July 5, 2025. Now part of the official Nouns collection — part of the infinite artwork forever on Ethereum. Every Noun generated with this head carries Nadiecito's pixel art in the protocol. ⌐◨-◨

Drawing Jam: WTF is ¿Nounish? — Cervezas Kalima, Cali

20+ local artists gathered to learn about Nouns, explore what "Nounish" means, and participate in the open call for the upcoming Feria Nounish 2.0. Artists drew in pairs with competitive prompts — winners chose between a beer or a pizza slice 🍕🍻.
